About BELIEVERS' CHAPEL OF POWER

Our Journey of Faith

BELIEVERS' CHAPEL OF POWER, also known as CHRIST OUR HOPE, began with a humble gathering of believers driven by a shared vision for spiritual growth and community impact. Founded in 2008, our church has grown from a small prayer group into a vibrant spiritual home for many.

Over the years, we have witnessed countless lives transformed by the power of God's Word and Spirit. Our journey has been marked by unwavering faith, dedication to service, and a deep commitment to spreading the Gospel. We cherish our past, celebrate our present, and look forward to a future filled with even greater impact.

We invite you to be a part of our continuing story as we strive to fulfill God's purpose for our lives and community.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is to redeem the hearts of men and women back to God by awakening a deep, personal relationship with Him through the truth of His word. In alignment with Hebrews 10:36 "For you have need of patience, that, after you have done the will of God, you might receive the promise", we seek to guide people into living a life of obedience, patience, and purpose, rooted in God’s will.

Our Mission

Our mission is to win souls for Christ our Lord through the preaching of the word of truth, to deliver those in spiritual bondage, and to declare the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, as referenced in Luke 4:18–19 (“He gave me gifts”). We are committed to helping those in need through our charity organization, taking the praises of the Lord to the whole world as we believe strongly in praise and worship, building ministers for the end-time revivals, giving hope to the hopeless, and ultimately, making heaven.

Our Core Values

Faith

We believe in the power of God's Word and trust in His unfailing promises.

Community

We foster a welcoming and supportive environment where everyone belongs.

Service

We are committed to serving God and humanity with compassion and dedication.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all we do, honoring God with our best.

Discipleship

We are dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth and equipping believers.

Impact

We aim to make a tangible, positive difference in our local and global communities.
