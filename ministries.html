Our Ministries

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of our youngest members in a fun, safe, and engaging environment. We believe in laying a strong biblical foundation from an early age, helping children understand God's love and His plan for their lives.

Through age-appropriate lessons, creative activities, and loving guidance, we aim to instill Christian values, encourage fellowship, and inspire a lifelong relationship with Jesus Christ. Our dedicated team of volunteers is committed to providing a positive and impactful experience for every child.

Learn More for Kids

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry at BELIEVERS' CHAPEL OF POWER is a dynamic and vibrant community designed to empower teenagers to navigate life's challenges with faith and purpose. We provide a supportive space where young people can grow in their relationship with God, build meaningful friendships, and discover their unique gifts.

Our programs include engaging Bible studies, interactive discussions, fun social events, and opportunities for service. We aim to equip our youth to be strong leaders, compassionate individuals, and impactful agents of change in their schools, homes, and communities.

Discover Youth Programs

Worship Ministry

Our Worship Ministry leads the congregation into the presence of God through heartfelt praise and worship. We believe that worship is a powerful expression of our love and devotion to God, and it creates an atmosphere for spiritual breakthrough and encounter.

Comprising talented musicians, vocalists, and technical teams, this ministry strives for excellence in leading worship services that are Spirit-filled and inspiring. We invite you to join us as we lift our voices and hearts in adoration to our Creator.

Join the Worship Team

Outreach Ministry

The Outreach Ministry of BELIEVERS' CHAPEL OF POWER is the hands and feet of Christ in our community and beyond. We are passionate about demonstrating God's love through practical acts of service, compassion, and evangelism.

From local community events to global mission trips, we actively seek opportunities to meet physical and spiritual needs, share the hope of the Gospel, and make a tangible difference in the lives of others. Join us as we extend God's love to the world.

Get Involved in Outreach

Men's Ministry

Our Men's Ministry is dedicated to building strong, godly men who are leaders in their homes, churches, and communities. We provide a supportive brotherhood where men can grow in their faith, share life experiences, and encourage one another to live out biblical principles.

Through regular gatherings, Bible studies, and fellowship events, we aim to equip men to fulfill their God-given potential and to be positive role models for the next generation.

Join Men's Ministry

Women's Ministry

The Women's Ministry at BELIEVERS' CHAPEL OF POWER is a vibrant sisterhood committed to empowering women to live purposeful, fulfilling, and Christ-centered lives. We create a nurturing environment where women can connect, grow in their faith, and support one another through all seasons of life.

Our ministry offers Bible studies, fellowship events, workshops, and opportunities for mentorship. We aim to inspire women to embrace their unique calling, develop their spiritual gifts, and make a significant impact in their families, church, and wider community.
