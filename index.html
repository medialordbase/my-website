About Us

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to redeem the hearts of men and women back to God by awakening a deep, personal relationship with Him through the truth of His word. In alignment with Hebrews 10:36 "For you have need of patience, that, after you have done the will of God, you might receive the promise", we seek to guide people into living a life of obedience, patience, and purpose, rooted in God’s will.

Our mission is to win souls for Christ our Lord through the preaching of the word of truth, to deliver those in spiritual bondage, and to declare the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, as referenced in Luke 4:18–19 (“He gave me gifts”). We are committed to helping those in need through our charity organization, taking the praises of the Lord to the whole world as we believe strongly in praise and worship, building ministers for the end-time revivals, giving hope to the hopeless, and ultimately, making heaven.
